Back at the church, walk towards the village, and a minute's stroll round the corner you will find Tewdric's Well on the left (Photo). Peering over the fence to the back of it, you can look down the stream you walked along a short while ago (VR View). This stream (when it flows) rises just behind the conifers next to the well. There is, conveniently, some wood over it (Photo), however, this is unlikely to be the original spring, as the gully it starts in is too deep, and the most detailed Ordnance Survey maps show it as a resurgence of a spring further up the hill.

It has also to be noted that the well itself doesn't entirely "run clear" - indeed, if Tewdric stopped there for a rest now the smell would probably have him pushing up daisys.
