O'Leary, J. (Ed.) (1880) The Most Ancient Lives Of Saint Patrick; Including The Life By Jocelin, Hitherto Unpublished In America, And His Extant Writings. Illustrated With The Most Ancient Engravings Of Our Great National Saint; With A Preface And Chronological Table. P. J. Kenedy, New York, pp.45 [online]

Includes: The Confession of St Patrick (455 CE); The Epistle to Coroticus (456 CE); The Metrical Life of St Patrick by St Fiech (493 CE); The Tripartite Life of St Patrick by St McEvin (510 CE); and The Life of St Patrick by Jocelyn (1185 CE).
