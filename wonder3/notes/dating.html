An overview of the dating evidence, which is largely coin-based and continues through to at least 470 CE can be found in Snyder*. Essentially the last datable coin is a 388-402 CE "House of Theodosius" coin, which is in association with pottery of a similar date. However, there are three well-worn pavements in the temples' inner precinct above this, suggesting continued use, possibly beyond the 6th C*
