In each case the locations taken are either those suggested by a number of exegetists, sites locally connected with the Wonders, or, when very desperate, the lone suggestion of anyone who cares to make one. While specifics can be found in the notes for each Wonder, it should generally be noted that the attribution of 'Dark Age' names to modern locations has been a study rampant with misuse and over-interpretation (see, for critiques, Newell*, Bromwich*, or Thomas Green's* note, on the fight to host Arthur's battles). Good online Gazetteers for 'Dark Age' Britain are Matthews* and Snyder*.
