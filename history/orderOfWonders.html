Sketchy at the moment, but it appears there are two versions here, including the following wonders: Version A, Wonder 1) The lake "lumonoi", with islands, which is as we have, and Version B, Wonders 1) Cheddar hole, 2) Rolendriht (Rollright stones?) 3) The White Horse (presumable Uffington: if, as the librarian claims, this section was added as late as the 17th C. this could have been another horse, however most were carved in the 18-19th C.) and 13) Stonehenge.
