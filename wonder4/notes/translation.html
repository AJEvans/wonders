Translation for this site. The original was Mommsen*: Quartum miraculum est: fontes in eadem inveniuntur de salo, a quibus fontibus sal coquitur: inde diversa cibaria saliuntur et non prope sunt mari, sed de terra emergunt.

Translation variations:

Färber & Irwin* have "The fourth wonder is the fountain of salt [which is] there.[end]"
Morris* has "the salt springs found there".
